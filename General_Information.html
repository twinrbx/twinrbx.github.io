1.1 IntroductionðŸ”—

Hi. My name is Twinqle, and I have been on Roblox since 2016. I am primarily involved with the sword fighting and theatre communities on the platform.

In 2020, I made a game called Sword Fight and Flex Your Time (commonly known as "SFAFYT"). It has accumulated 17 million visits over 4 years. At this time, SFAFYT is not actively maintained or updated.

1.2 Contact InformationðŸ”—

To contact me, message me via direct message on Discord.

Why didn’t you respond to my message?
If you say something along the lines of “can I ask a question,” I will probably ignore you. This is a practice called NoHello. You can learn more about it here.You should instead ask the question immediately, because it saves both of us time.

If you ask a question about how to use one of my products, I may defer you to the instructions provided with the product. This means I believe your question is answered in the instructions, which, frankly, are written with clarity in mind.

To be clear, if you ask a question about the nature of one of my products, I will usually respond. It’s reasonable to ask about a product’s features or limits before deciding to purchase.
